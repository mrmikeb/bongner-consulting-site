What we do

Independent consulting to get the best‑fit overhead equipment

Requirements & Application Definition

Document duty cycle, load profiles, headroom, span, clearances, environment, controls, and integration points to avoid over‑ or under‑specifying.

Specifications & Drawings Review

Develop or review technical specs, layout, runway/structure considerations, and interface details to align with performance and budget.

Vendor Sourcing & Evaluation

RFP/RFQ preparation, objective bid comparisons, lifecycle cost analysis, and reference checks—manufacturer‑agnostic.

Compliance & Risk

Align designs and procedures with applicable standards and good practice (e.g., OSHA 1910.179, ASME B30 series, CMAA). Provide hazard identification and mitigation guidance.

Implementation Support

Vendor coordination, FAT/SAT checklists, installation oversight, testing, and acceptance criteria to ensure systems operate as intended.

Training & Lifecycle

Operator/maintenance awareness, inspection planning, spare parts strategy, and upgrade paths for reliability and uptime.

Legal support

Expert witness & failure analysis for overhead cranes

For attorneys and insurers, Bongner Consulting Services delivers clear, factual analysis of overhead crane incidents and component failures. Typical work products include:

  • Evidence review (designs, maintenance/inspection records, operating data, photos/video)
  • Site inspections and documentation protocols
  • Root cause and contributing factor analysis
  • Standards and conformance assessment
  • Technical reports, demonstratives, and testimony preparation

Disclaimer: Consulting and expert witness services provide technical opinions and do not constitute legal advice.

How we work

5) Implementation

What we do

Independent consulting to get the best-fit overhead equipment

Requirements & Application Definition

Document duty cycle, load profiles, headroom, span, clearances, environment, controls, and integration points to avoid over- or under-specifying.

Specifications & Drawings Review

Develop or review technical specs, layouts, runway and structure considerations, and interface details to align with performance and budget.

Vendor Sourcing & Evaluation

RFP/RFQ preparation, objective bid comparisons, lifecycle cost analysis, and reference checks — manufacturer-agnostic.

Compliance & Risk

Align designs and procedures with applicable standards and good practices, including OSHA 1910.179, ASME B30 series, and CMAA.

Implementation Support

Vendor coordination, FAT/SAT checklists, installation oversight, testing, and acceptance criteria to ensure systems operate as intended.

Training & Lifecycle

Operator and maintenance awareness, inspection planning, spare parts strategy, and upgrade paths for reliability and uptime.

Legal support

Expert witness & failure analysis for overhead cranes

For attorneys and insurers, Bongner Consulting Services delivers clear, factual analysis of overhead crane incidents and component failures.

  • Evidence review including designs, maintenance records, inspection records, photos, and video
  • Site inspections and documentation protocols
  • Root cause and contributing factor analysis
  • Standards and conformance assessment
  • Technical reports, demonstratives, and testimony preparation

How we work

Practical, transparent engagement model

1) Discovery

Clarify objectives, constraints, stakeholders, and success criteria.

2) Requirements

Quantify loads, cycles, geometry, environment, controls, and safety needs.

3) Specifications

Develop vendor-neutral specs and drawings; align on acceptance tests.

4) Sourcing

Prepare RFQs, evaluate bids, negotiate scope, and assess lifecycle cost.

5) Implementation

Oversee installation, testing, and punch-list items through safe handover.

6) Support

Post-install review, training, and inspection or maintenance planning.
